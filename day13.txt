Step 1: Log in to Azure Portal
•Go to Azure Portal and sign in.

Step 2: Create the First Container
•Search for "Container Instances" and click Create.
->Fill in these details:
•Name: Give a name for the container.
•Image: Enter the Docker image name (e.g., nginx:latest or httpd:latest).
•Ports: Open port 80.
•Click Review + Create, then Create.

Step 3: Create the Second Container
•Repeat Step 2 to create the second container with a different name or image.
•Use the same steps to configure the image and ports.

Step 4: Access the Containers
•Go to the Overview page of each container.
•Copy their IP addresses and test them in your browser.
